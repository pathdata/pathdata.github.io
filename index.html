About Me

I am a computational scientist and biomedical researcher with a strong background in cellular and pan cancer tissue phenotypes. In my current role as a senior scientific officer, I work on image-guided selection of targeted spatial regions based on artificial intelligence (AI) and integrating multi-modal information from histology, molecular classification, and radiology.

My expertise spans developing visionary packages to encapsulate ecological metrics and study their association with clinical phenotypes as an end-to-end workflow. I specialize in creating translational and reproducible workflows that address gaps and challenges in mapping multi-modal images and analyzing the tumor immune microenvironment.
